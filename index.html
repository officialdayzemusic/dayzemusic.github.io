Artist Bio

DayzeMusic blends melodic rap and ethereal pop soundscapes, creating music that’s both emotional and atmospheric. With a growing fanbase and an evolving sound, Dayze continues to push boundaries in modern independent music.

Based in the U.S., he’s performed at notable regional events and continues to craft tracks that explore themes of resilience, love, and personal growth.
